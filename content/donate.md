why_title: Why Donate?
why_image: /assets/images/donate-side.jpg

why_text: |
  While our success is only possible through the generosity of our volunteers devoting many hours to our Stars, your financial support plays a vital role in sustaining and growing our program.

  Donations help cover the cost of ice time, adaptive skating equipment, and program operations, ensuring that every skater has access to a safe and supportive environment. Your support allows us to continue expanding opportunities for individuals with disabilities to build confidence, independence, and community through skating.

  Every contribution, no matter the size, directly impacts the lives of our skaters and their families. Thank you for supporting all that we do!
